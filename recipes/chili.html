Chili

Chili is one of my favorite staples. It's super great to make in large quantities and freeze for lazy meals. I like to put lots of cheese in mine and use dark kidney beans. Here's my go-to recipe:

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 2 yellow onions
  • 6 garlic cloves
  • 3 large jalape√±o peppers
  • 3 serano peppers OR 2-3 habaneros
  • 2 cans diced tomatoes
  • 1 can tomato sauce
  • 3 cans dark kidney beans
  • 1/4 cup chili powder
  • 1 tsp salt

Steps:

  1. dice up onions, peppers and cloves
  2. brown the meat
  3. put all everything in crock pot
  4. let cook for several (8-10) hours
  5. you have a bunch of chili, it's great!
