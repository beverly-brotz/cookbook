Meatloaf

I used to hate meatloaf growing up. But I have discovered that the only reason for this was that my mom made the worst meatloaf ever. I'm pretty sure it consisted purely of stale bread, ketchup and meat. I prefer to use seasonings and this so that it actually tastes good! Here's how I make it:

Ingredients:

  • 2 lbs ground beef
  • 1 med onion, finely chopped
  • 2 large eggs
  • 3 garlic cloves, minced
  • 3 Tbsp ketchup
  • 1 tbsp worctershire
  • 3/4 cup panko breadcrumbs
  • 1/3 cup milk
  • 1/2 tsp salt, or to taste
  • 1 1/2 tsp Italian seasoning

Steps:

  1. Preheat oven to 375°F
  2. mix up all the ingredients, keeping the sauce separate. You can put it in a bowl and use a masher, your hands, or put it in a bag and knead it. However you go about it is up to you.
  3. bake for 40 minutes
  4. take it out and spread the sauce out over the top
  5. bake an additional 15-20 minutes, or until internal temp reaches 160°F
  6. cool 10 minutes before slicing and serving
